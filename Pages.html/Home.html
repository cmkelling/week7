Chore Chart and Reward Tracker

The Why


Getting things done around the house can be hard, so creating a rewards system helps motivate you to clean and organize!




The How


On the next two pages are chores that need to be done, either daily, weekly, or monthly as well as a tiered table of rewards that can be gotten for completing chore(s) based on the amount of time/ effort spent on them.
A form is available to submit completed chores as well as links leading to each pages for easy navigation.
